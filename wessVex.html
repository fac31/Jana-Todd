Welcome About Me CV Wess Vex

Wess Vex was and remains to be a creative outlet for myself so that I can continue showcasing my entrepreneurial, economic, and artistic passions. I began the brand almost 3 years ago during the pandemic as a way for me to express myself in a field I am passionate about. Wess Vex is now stocked in 3 stores across 3 different countries and has seen great success in pushing the 'up-cycling' & 0-waste narrative for the arts & apparel industry.
